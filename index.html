Here are some basic question about Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

1.What is Swachh Bharat Abhiyan ?

Swachh Bharat Mission, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, or Clean India Mission is a country-wide campaign initiated by the Government of India in 2014 to eliminate open defecation and improve solid waste management. It is really important nowadays .

2.When was this occasion introduced ?

It was first introduced in 2014 To accelerate the efforts to achieve universal sanitation coverage and to put the focus on sanitation, the Prime Minister of India had launched the Swachh Bharat Mission on 2nd October 2014.

3.Where was it introduced ?

It was first started in New delhi and then spreaded over india.
